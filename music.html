Four-plus decades, Jeremy from Boise was an artist. They were from Boise, Idaho

While still an artist, we are now more than that as well. If you'd like to collaborate on music, there are some tracks I had begun and don't quite know how to finish. I would be very honored and thrilled to have your contribution or hear your interpretation to my original idea.

As you may guess by listening, perfection is not the objective and you could use your phone to record vocal takes or remix stuff using free software. The pieces are streamable in the player below. You can download the track at the SoundCloud or contact me here to see if there are stems available.
